Cinnamon Tea

Ingredients Required:

  • Cinnamon Sticks
  • Honey
  • Lemon
  • Mint Leaves
  • water

Process of Making:

  1. Take a sauce pan and pour one glass of water in it.
  2. Heat it for 2 minutes and wait until water comes to boil.
  3. Now add two-inch long cinnamon sticks and simmer it for few minutes.
  4. After a while, the colour of water changes and becomes flavourful.
  5. Turn off the flame and filter the contents in to a glass.
  6. Add two teaspoons of Honey in it and squeeze a medium lemon slice in the tea.
  7. Finally garnish the tea by putting two-three mint leaves on it.
  8. Now your fabulous cinnamon tea is ready to be sipped.
